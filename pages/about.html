About Me

Who I am and what drives me

Building intelligent systems with curiosity, clarity, and purpose

I'm Amulya Shetty, a Software Engineer focused on building AI-powered products, automation systems, and scalable data platforms. My work sits at the intersection of AI, backend engineering, and cloud infrastructure where I design systems that can reason, automate, and operate reliably in real-world environments.

I enjoy turning complex, ambiguous problems into structured, production-ready solutions. My experience spans GenAI applications, agentic workflows, RAG systems, ETL pipelines, and cloud-native platforms, with work ranging from intelligent assistants and recommendation engines to large-scale data processing and workflow automation. What draws me to this space is the challenge of combining modern AI capabilities with strong engineering foundations to create systems that are not only innovative, but dependable, scalable, and genuinely useful.

Currently, I'm part of Flexera's AI Centre of Excellence, where I work on AI-driven automation across enterprise domains, building multi-agent frameworks, intelligent workflows, and cloud-based solutions that help teams move faster and make better decisions. I've worked across technologies such as Python, AWS, Databricks, Terraform, LLM frameworks, and data engineering systems, with a strong focus on building software that delivers measurable impact.

What drives me is the belief that great engineering goes beyond shipping features. It's about understanding problems deeply, making thoughtful design decisions, and building systems that are elegant under the hood and reliable in practice. I care about clean architecture, scalable design, meaningful automation, and technology that solves real problems, not just impressive demos.
